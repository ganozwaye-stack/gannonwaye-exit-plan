# OAuth Connectors

> 6 OAuth integrations to re-authorize on the new platform

## Instagram Business
- **Type:** `instagram`
- **Scopes:** instagram_business_basic, instagram_business_content_publish
- **Action:** Re-authorize on Emergent

## Slack
- **Type:** `slack`
- **Scopes:** chat:write, users:read.email, users:read
- **Action:** Re-authorize on Emergent

## Google Drive
- **Type:** `googledrive`
- **Scopes:** email
- **Action:** Re-authorize on Emergent

## Google Calendar
- **Type:** `googlecalendar`
- **Scopes:** calendar.events, email
- **Action:** Re-authorize on Emergent

## Gmail
- **Type:** `gmail`
- **Scopes:** gmail.send, email
- **Action:** Re-authorize on Emergent

## Google Sheets
- **Type:** `googlesheets`
- **Scopes:** spreadsheets, drive.file, email
- **Action:** Re-authorize on Emergent

## Setup Instructions

1. On Emergent, go to Integrations → OAuth Connectors
2. For each connector, click "Connect" and authorize with the respective Google/Slack/Instagram/TikTok account
3. Ensure redirect URIs point to the Emergent platform
4. Test each connection before going live
